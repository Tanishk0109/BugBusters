3. Basic Vocabulary

Let's learn some essential signs for everyday communication. Remember, facial expressions and body language are crucial in sign language!

Hello

Wave your hand, palm facing out, near your forehead.

Thank You

Touch your chin with your fingertips, then move your hand outward.

Please

Rub your chest in a circular motion with your palm.

Sorry

Make a fist and rub it in a circular motion over your chest.

Yes

Make your hand into a fist and nod it like a nodding head.

No

Make your first two fingers like scissors and tap them together.

Practice Tips

  • Practice fingerspelling your name and common words you use daily.
  • Use a mirror to check your hand shapes and facial expressions.
  • Try to incorporate the basic vocabulary into your daily routine.
  • Watch videos of native signers to improve your understanding of natural signing flow.
  • Practice with a friend or family member to make learning more interactive and fun.
